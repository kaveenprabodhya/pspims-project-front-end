Uses of Coconut & Copra Products

Coconut
  • Fresh coconut water: Hydrating natural drink
  • Coconut meat: Cooking, snacks, and desserts
  • Coconut oil: Cooking and skincare
  • Coconut husk and coir: Ropes, mats, and gardening
Copra
  • Raw material for coconut oil extraction
  • Used in soap and cosmetics manufacturing
  • Animal feed ingredient
  • Biofuel and charcoal production
Coconut Vinegar
  • Natural food preservative and flavor enhancer
  • Used in cooking and salad dressings
  • Traditional medicinal uses
  • Eco-friendly cleaning agent
Other Products
  • Coconut sugar: Natural sweetener
  • Coconut charcoal: Fuel and filtration
  • Coconut shell crafts and activated carbon
  • Coconut-based beverages and snacks
